Coconut Milk

Packaging Options:

Coconut milk is the liquid that comes from the grated white meat of a matured Coconut. The colour and rich taste of the milk can be attributed to the high Oil content. Most of the fat is saturated fat. Coconut milk is a very popular food ingredient used in the world.Coconut milk has become a highlight of many cuisines in the tropical and subtropical countries where they are grown. Coconut milk is a fantastic dairy free alternative, popular in curry dishes.

Coconut Cream

Packaging Options:

Organic Coconut Cream is the pure natural extract of fresh, mature coconut meat at 24% fat content. The process used preserves the freshness, natural flavor and aroma, and quality of coconut cream of fresh coconuts. It is a smooth-flowing liquid and creamy white in color. No preservatives or artificial flavoring are added. Coconut cream is a main cooking ingredient for interesting Asian dishes such as curries, desserts, and other delicacies such as puddings and rice cakes.

Creamed Coconut

Packaging Options:

Creamed Coconut can also be called as Coconut Concentrate which is the unsweetened dehydrated fresh meat of a mature fruit of Coconut ground to a semi-solid white creamy paste. Creamed Coconut is suitable for consumption without the need of further processing. Do not confuse between Creamed Coconut and Coconut Cream as Coconut Cream has a maximum moisture content of about 74% whereas Creamed Coconut has moisture of only 3% maximum, and is naturally known as Coconut Concentrate.

Coconut Flour

Packaging Options:

Coconut flour is obtained from the whitish kernel residue left after the extraction of Virgin Coconut Oil and has a potential application in high protein-fiber enriched food products. Coconut Flour is known to have a strong sweet coco-nutty aroma. It has a white cream color and sometimes a few brown spots from coconut meat brown skin which is natural. It also has a high dietary fiber content which is 3 to 4 times more that the wheat flour, Soy flour and Rice bran.

RBD Coconut Oil

Packaging Options:

RBD (Refined, Bleached, Deodorised) coconut oil is produced by refining crude coconut oil. Our refinery plant uses a 100% physical process and does not add any chemicals. Without any coconut flavour or aroma, RBD coconut oil makes a very versatile oil. It is the most commonly used commercial cooking oil worldwide, it is also used for various other cosmetic and pharmaceutical purposes.
